About the Authors

Jack Self

Jack Self is actively involved in working with companies to reduce their supply chain costs. Most recently, Jack was the Vice-President of Sourcing for Labrie Enviroquip. He has extensive international experience with procurement negotiations. Jack has an aerospace engineering degree, an MBA, a Diploma in Computer Science, and a Master in Finance.

Valérie Demers

Valérie Demers specializes in mathematical models and numerical algorithms. She has built cost models and software applications for supply chains in various industries (aerospace, mobile equipment, glass) for clients such as Bombardier Aerospace and Labrie Enviroquip. She has a Master of Science in Mathematics.
