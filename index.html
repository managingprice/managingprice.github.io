This must-have guide for procurement professionals presents a multidisciplinary approach to supplier negotiations. The tools, techniques and information in this book will enable you to:

  • Master a proven process for negotiating with suppliers.
  • Go from market analysis to contract completion in 4 logical steps.
  • Ensure fair pricing on all purchased commodities.
  • Create effective strategies for supply chain cost management.
  • Secure the best price and a great supplier relationship.

He has extensive international experience with procurement negotiations. Jack has an aerospace engineering degree, an MBA, a Diploma in Computer Science and a Master in Finance.
